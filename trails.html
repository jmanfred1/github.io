Get out and explore the world around you.

Explore Trails was founded back in 2006 with the mission to get as many people out and experiencing the world around them. It works by using your location as a means to find the best trails right in your backyard! With combinations of user reviews and geotagging we are able to set you up with great new hikes just minutes from home. Visiting a new town? Our hidden hotspots feature analyzes the best user reviews and least trafficed areas to get you out on the best kept secret by locals!
