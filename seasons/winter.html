Winter: The Ranch.

The cast of That 70's Show is back in this Netflix based sitcom. Starrting Ashton Kutcher and Sam Elliott, follow the Bennett family as they deal with the up's and downs of small-town, family-ranch life. This won our winter selection as it takes place in the snowy mountains of Colorado. The Ranch will have you curled up by the fire, glass of whiskey in hand, and enjoying cold evenings indoors.
