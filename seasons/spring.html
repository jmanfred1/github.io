Spring: Parks and Recreation.

Follow Leslie Knope and her coworkers in the Parks department as they navigate civil bureaucracy in this NBC documentary style sitcom. Amy Poehler and Nick Offerman star in this series. We chose it for our spring viewing for its emphasis on the parks department of Pawnee, IN. The consistent time outside, green landscapes, and quirky comedy are great for preparing us to treat yo self this summer.

Happy Viewing!
