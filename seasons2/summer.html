Summer: New Girl.

New Girl is the easy winner for our summer sitcom selection. Taking place in sunny Los Angeles, Jess, Nick, Schmidt, and Winston navigate life through triumphs, failures, and relationships. The overall brightness and lighthearted comedy are great for the season. Make sure to coincide the true American episodes with the 4th of July! JFK! FDR!
