Winterfest at Hills

Winterfest is a special time for Hill's Resort. The months of March and April are time for growth and preparedness for the comming season. As our last weekend open for the year we need to clear out the cupboards. Enjoy half off lodging, beer, wine, and select menu items. We also bring in a DJ, the Snowshoe Volleyball Finals, and the Vintage Snowmobile Race Finals!
