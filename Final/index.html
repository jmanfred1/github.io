Winter at the Lake

Summer and Fall aren't the only times to enjoy the beauty of Priest Lake!

"Sensational in all seasons", as our Chamber of Commerce says! In the winter, Priest Lake transorms from the hiker, watersports, and angler's paradise to one for snowmobilers, cross country skiiers, snowshoers, and winter enthusiats!

Tradition is key in the culture of Priest Lake, join us for some of the best community events of the year. Soak in the view at Hill's Resort on New Years Eve. Featuring specialty cocktails, a custom menu, and hors d'oeuvres in our top rated restaurant and bar. Thorw a pitch and get your team together for the 52nd annual Snowshoe Softball tournament at Priest Lake Golf Course. Get your adrenalline pumping on the snow banks at the 7th annual Vintage Snowmobile Races. Finally, say goodbye to the winter season with some Snowshoe Volleyball finals, DJ, and half priced beer at Winterfest.
