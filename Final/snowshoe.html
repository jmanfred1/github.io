52nd Annual Snowshoe Softball Tournament

Each winter, 12 teams from around the northwest gather to play in the annual Priest Lake Snowshoe Softball tournament at the Priest Lake Golf Course. Teams are built of a local business sponsor an a group of individals playing. Custom plastic snowshoes are provided along with pink softballs to be seen in the snow. These weekends are usually a great mix of competiton on the field, beer in the duggouts, and music at Hill's.
