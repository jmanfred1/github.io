Vintage Snowmobile Races

Even with the longstanding winter traditons of the lake, there is always room for something new. 7 years ago the vintage snowmobile races began on the USFS Airstrip and Ranger Station on Hwy 57. The event has grown in popularity and now attracts over 3,000 spectators and 200 racers. All sleds must be manufactured before 1985, and racers compete in different engine and age brackets. Spectators usually arrive early to secure their tailgate spot on the snowbank, be sure to bring a grill, firepit, and dress warm.
