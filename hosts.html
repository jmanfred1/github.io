Web Hosting Project

BlueHost

BlueHost.com is one of the 20 largest web hosting services, they offer various packages for businesses of all sizes.

Plans

all plans include unmetered bandwidth, standard perfomance, and free SSL certificates.

Plan 1: is a basic plan for small businesses just starting their websites.

What it offers...

-You are able to host one website.

-You will have 50 GB of website storage.

-You are allowed 1 domain, 5 parked domains, and 25 sub domains.

-You are allotted 5 email accounts, each with 100 mb of storage.

-Cost is $2.95 for the first month, 7.99 afterwards.

Plans 2 and 3: Are Plus and Choice Plus plans designed for large or growing businesses.

What they offer...

-You are able to host one website.

-You have unlimited website storage.

-You are allowed 1 domain and unlimited parked and sub domains.

-You are allotted unlimited email accounts, each with unlimited storage.

-Cost is $5.45 for the first month, 10.99 for Plus and 14.99 for Choice Plus afterwards.

- *Choice Plus offers several added security features, valued at over $80 per year.

HostGator

HostGator.com caters to every business from small to enterpirse according to their website. They also offer free domain for an entire year.

Plans

all plans include a free domain and SSL Certification.

Plan 1: the Hatchling plan is a limited plan for businesses just getting started.

What it offers...

-A single domain.

-You will have unlimited pages.

-You are given unlimited storage.

-You are allotted unlimited email accounts.

-Cost is $2.75 for the first three years.

Plan 2: the Baby plan is the reccomended plan by HostGator.

What it offers...

-Unlimited domains.

-You will have unlimited bandwidth.

-Opportunity to have a private SSL.

-You are allotted unlimited email accounts.

-Cost is $3.95 for the first three years.

Plan 3: the Business plan is the largest plan offered by HostGator.

What it offers...

-Everything in plans 1&2.

-Dedicated IP.

-Free Postitive SSL Upgrade.

-Additional SEO marketing tools.

-Cost is $5.95 for the first three years.

SquareSpace

SquareSpace.com offers the most options for companies getting ready to start their webside. Not only will they host the site, but they will also help you build the site.

Plans

all plans include a custom website templates and site builder, making this the easiest site for beginners.

Plan 1: the Personal plan is great for starting a blog or portfolio.

What it offers...

-Free Domain, SSL, and unlimited bandwidth.

-Two contributors are allowed.

-Mobile sites available.

-SEO tools to increase site popularity.

-Cost is $12 per month.

Plan 2: the Business plan is the most popular plan.

What it offers...

-Unlimited contributors.

-Email powered by Google.

-Customization with Java.

-$100 Google adwords credit.

-Cost is $18 per month.

Plan 3: the Basic and Advanced commerce plans are available for people looking for online retail outlets.

What it offers...

-Everything in plans 1&2.

-0% transaction fee on purchases (3% in plan 2).

-Point of sale support for in store.

-Customer accounts for when they revist the page.

-Cost is $26 and $40 per month.

*Advanced Commerce offers the ability to sell subscritions, advanced shipping, and advanced discounts.
